About

Hi! Thanks for visiting my personal website. Here, you can find an assortment of my past projects, as well as a taste of what I'm up to and interested in today.

A little bit about me: I'm a UPenn graduate with a bachelors degree in Bioengineering and a master's degree in Systems Engineering, currently working at the intersection of machine learning and healthcare. I am currently located in the New York area, and am a lover of soccer, music, chess, and traveling.

Past Projects

An Evaluation of Abstractive and Extractive Deep Learning NLP Text Summarization Techniques

Drafted a research paper on a topic of choice for a graudate-level deep learning course at the University of Pennsylvania. Main contributions included implementing beam search decoder as an improvement to Bahdanau's attention-based neural machine translation model fine-tuned on a CNN news articles dataset, as well as introducing a question-answering evaluation mechanism to evaluate and benchmark performance

Implementing a Neural Network From Scratch

An exercise in creating and training a neural network from scratch, including manual computation and caching of values from forward process for use in backpropagation, using only pure python and numpy. Compared performance to a PyTorch network

Predicting Inpatient Length of Stay at Hospitals

Utilized > 2 million rows of patient data to uncover clinically relevant insights and predict patient length of stay using only data present at time of patient admittance. Makes progress towards improving hospital management efficiency
